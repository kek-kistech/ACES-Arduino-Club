# ACES-Arduino-Club
Welcome to the **Arduino Club Repo**! This is our central hub for sharing resources, projects, and learning materials related to Arduino and embedded systems. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced maker, you'll find something useful here. 
 ## 📚 About the Club.
We are a community of students and hobbyists passionate about electronics, coding, and hardware tinkering. Our mission is to: 
- Learn and experiment with Arduino microcontrollers
- Build fun and practical projects
- Share knowledge and resources
- Collaborate on open-source hardware/software initiatives

📅 Club Activities
- Weekly workshops on Arduino basics
- Monthly project showcases
- Hackathons and competitions
- Collaborative builds
